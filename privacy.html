Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

CleanTab is built with privacy as a core principle. This policy explains how the extension handles your information.

Data collection

CleanTab does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. The extension uses no analytics and no tracking.

Local storage

The extension stores the following locally on your device only:

  • Your cleaning preferences (selected items, time range)
  • Your automatic cleaning settings
  • Your protected sites list (whitelist)
  • Your interface preferences (language, theme)
  • Cleaning statistics (count, categories, streak)

This data never leaves your browser.

Permissions

  • browsingData — to delete the browsing data you select
  • storage — to save your preferences locally
  • alarms — to schedule automatic cleaning
  • notifications — to notify you when an automatic clean is done

Third parties

CleanTab does not share any data with third parties because it does not collect any data in the first place.
